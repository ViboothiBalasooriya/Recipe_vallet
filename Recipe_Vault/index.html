Recipe of the Day

Signature Garden
Harvest Bowl

A vibrant blend of seasonal greens, roasted root vegetables, and our secret zesty tahini dressing.

Ingredients
  • 2 cups Organic Kale
  • 1 Roasted Sweet Potato
  • 1/2 Avocado (Sliced)
  • 1/4 cup Quinoa
  • Zesty Tahini Dressing
Quick Info
⏱ 20m prep 🔥 350 kcal 🥗 Vegan
Steps to Perfection
01. Massage the kale with a touch of olive oil until tender and bright green.
02. Arrange roasted sweet potatoes and cooked quinoa over the base.
03. Drizzle generously with tahini dressing and top with fresh avocado.
